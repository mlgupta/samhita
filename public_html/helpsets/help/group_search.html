How to Search Group?
  • Enter search condition in 'Search by Group Name' either full Group Name or the starting words can be entered to search for related Groups.
    (You can also use wild card characters '*' or '?'.)
  • The Group(s) will be displayed in the Group List which match the entered text.
  • If the search condition doesn't match,a page will be displayed with text 'There is no item to display'.
 
What is Group List ?
  • Group List consists of the groups of account holders in the application.
